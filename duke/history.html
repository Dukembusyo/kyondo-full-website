The Origins and Significance

The art of Kyondo weaving is deeply embedded in Kenyan traditions, particularly among the Kikuyu and Kamba communities. Historically, Kyondo baskets were not only functional but also served as symbols of cultural identity and craftsmanship. Discover the stories of how these traditions shaped the heritage we cherish today.
